## General Billing Questions

### What billing cycles are available?
We offer both monthly and annual billing cycles. Annual subscriptions include a discount of [X]% compared to monthly billing.

### When am I charged?
Your subscription is billed at the start of each billing cycle. For monthly plans, this is the same date each month. For annual plans, it's the same date each year.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
- Major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (Enterprise plans only)
- Regional payment methods (where available)

## Subscription Management

### How do I upgrade my plan?
1. Navigate to Billing Settings
2. Click "Upgrade Plan"
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the change

The price difference will be prorated for your current billing period.

### What happens when I downgrade?
When downgrading:
- Changes take effect at the end of current billing cycle
- Unused time on higher tier is credited
- Data remains accessible within new plan limits
- Custom features may be disabled

### How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Select "Subscription"
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Follow confirmation steps
